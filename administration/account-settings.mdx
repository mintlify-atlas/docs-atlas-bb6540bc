title: Account settings
description: Manage your personal profile, notification preferences, and account security settings.
---

Account settings let you update your personal information, control which notifications you receive, and manage your login security. Navigate to `/user-profile` to access these settings.

## Accessing account settings

Click your avatar or name in the top navigation bar and select **Configuración de Perfil**, or navigate directly to `/user-profile`.

## Settings tabs

The account settings page is organized into three tabs.

### Account (Cuenta)

The **Cuenta** tab (`AccountTab`) lets you update your personal profile information:

- Display name
- Profile photo
- Contact email
- Any other profile fields configured for your organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Account tab">
    Navigate to `/user-profile`. The **Cuenta** tab is active by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your information">
    Update any field you want to change. Required fields are marked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Guardar** to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Notifications

The **Notificaciones** tab (`NotificationTab`) controls which platform events trigger alerts for your account. Toggle individual notification types on or off based on your preferences.

Notification settings are stored per user and do not affect other users' preferences.

### Security

The **Seguridad** tab (`SecurityTab`) lets you update your account password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Security tab">
    Click **Seguridad** in the account settings tab bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    Provide your existing password to verify your identity before making changes. TMT uses `reauthenticateWithCredential` from the Firebase Auth SDK to re-authenticate you before accepting the new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter and confirm your new password">
    Type the new password and confirm it in the second field. Both values must match.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Guardar** to update your password. You will remain logged in after the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Store your new password securely before saving. TMT does not display the password after you save it, and account recovery requires access to your registered email address.
</Warning>
